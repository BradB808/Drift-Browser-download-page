Browsing, the way your head works

🗺️

An infinite canvas

Pan and pinch through your pages like a map. Zoom out and your session becomes a constellation of live thumbnails; dive in and every card is a full Chromium page — logins, video, web apps, everything.

🕸️

Trails, not history

Open a link in a new tab and Drift spawns a child card with a visible trail back to where you came from. Draw trails yourself by dragging between cards, and export any trail as a Markdown reading list.

▢

Zones keep projects together

Name a region of your canvas — “Trip planning”, “Job hunt”, “Biology” — pick its color, and move the whole thing at once. Zones even grow to fit whatever you put inside.

⛶

Focus, split, full screen

Double-click to focus a page, pin two side-by-side to compare, or take one page truly full-screen. Esc always floats you back out to the map.

★

Bookmarks & instant search

Star any page and find it again from the bookmarks shelf or the ⌘T palette. ⌘F searches every open card — matches light up right on the canvas.

🔒

Private by design

Your canvas, trails, and bookmarks are saved on your machine and nowhere else. No accounts, no sync servers, no browsing log. A fresh photo backdrop greets every launch.
