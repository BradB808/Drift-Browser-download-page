Questions,
answered

Is Drift free?

Yes. Drift is free and open source under the GPL-3.0 license. No accounts, no subscriptions, no upsells.

What is a spatial browser?

A browser that lays your pages out as cards on a zoomable canvas instead of a row of tabs, so your session looks like a map. Read the full explainer.

What does Drift run on?

macOS, on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs. A Windows build is coming soon.

Does Drift support Chrome extensions?

Yes. Install ad blockers and other extensions straight from the Chrome Web Store; their icons live in the Drift toolbar.

Does Drift track my browsing?

No. There are no accounts, no sync servers, and no browsing log. Your canvas and bookmarks stay on your machine.

What engine does Drift use?

Chromium, via Electron. Every card is a real sandboxed browser page, so logins, video, and web apps all work like you expect.
